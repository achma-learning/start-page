Daily Program

Internalize & master
  1. Seize the day 11 PM bedtime. 5 AM rise — early to bed and early to rise makes a man healthy, wealthy, and wise. This routine fuels body, mind, and spirit.
  2. Mental clarity & focus Set goals, keep a to-do list, visualize outcomes. Strengthen your focus, ignite your vision.
  3. Deep work immersion Dedicate 4 hours of focused work. Eliminate distractions. Each task builds mastery. I am immersed in my work, growing every moment.
  4. Power your body Complete 50 push-ups and squats. Strength in the body mirrors strength in the mind. A healthy body leads to a bright mind.
  5. Master your craft Invest 15–30 minutes daily in skill learning or Anki review. Small, consistent steps. Each day, you compound your knowledge and skills.
